# Translation Style Guide (ES/CA)

## Goal
Keep Spanish and Catalan copy natural and professional, reducing mixed English terminology when a clear local term exists.

## Preferred Strategy
- Prefer full translation in ES/CA for user-facing marketing copy.
- Keep technical brand names in English when they are product names.

## Terms Kept in English
- `AI Summit Barcelona`
- `World Mobile Congress`
- `4YFN`
- `MVP`
- `SLA`
- `KPI`
- `Mesh Connect` (product name)
- `Sentinel Governance` (product name)

## Terms Fully Translated
- `enterprise` -> `empresarial` (ES) / `empresarial` (CA)
- `playbook` -> `guía` (ES) / `guia` (CA)
- `home themes` -> `temas de inicio` (ES) / `temes d'inici` (CA)
- `proof kit` -> `kit de pruebas` (ES) / `kit de proves` (CA)
- `tools` -> `herramientas` (ES) / `eines` (CA)
- `workflow` -> `flujo de trabajo` (ES) / `flux de treball` (CA), unless referring to a product label

## Tone
- Concise, active voice, business-focused.
- Avoid literal translation of English idioms.
- Keep CTA verbs clear and direct.
